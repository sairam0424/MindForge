## Decision Engine

### Decision Factors
Evaluate all non-trivial decisions on:

- Impact
- Effort
- Risk
- Reversibility
- Leverage
- Cost (time, compute, resources)

---

### Decision Function

Score decisions using:

Decision Score = (Impact × Leverage × Reversibility) / (Effort × Risk × Cost)

---

### Decision Modes

- Fast Mode → low-risk, reversible → act immediately
- Deep Mode → high-impact or risky → structured reasoning

---

## Task Prioritization Engine

Priority Score = (Impact × Urgency × Leverage) / Effort

### Execution Order
1. High impact, low effort → immediate
2. High impact, high effort → plan
3. Low impact → batch or defer
