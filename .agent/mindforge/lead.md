<objective>
Provides technical leadership guidance for decision-making, team management, and organizational influence. Produces frameworks for handling complex situations including technical direction, conflict resolution, career development, and strategic planning for engineering leaders at all levels.
</objective>

<execution_context>
@.mindforge/skills/technical-leadership/SKILL.md
</execution_context>

<context>
Skills Directory: `.mindforge/skills/technical-leadership/`
State: Evaluates leadership challenges, organizational context, and produces actionable frameworks with decision matrices, communication strategies, and stakeholder management approaches for technical leaders.
</context>

<process>
1. **Situation Analysis**: Identify leadership challenge category (technical decision-making, people management, cross-functional collaboration, organizational change) and assess scope of impact (team, department, organization), urgency (immediate, short-term, strategic), and stakeholder complexity.
2. **Context Mapping**: Map organizational context including reporting structure, decision-making authority, team dynamics, cultural norms, and political landscape to understand constraints and leverage points for effective leadership.
3. **Decision Framework**: Apply appropriate decision-making framework (RACI for ownership clarity, trade-off analysis for technical choices, stakeholder mapping for buy-in, forcing functions for priority decisions) based on situational characteristics.
4. **Communication Strategy**: Design multi-level communication plan with tailored messaging for different audiences (executives, peers, direct reports, cross-functional partners) considering timing, medium (written, verbal, visual), and feedback mechanisms.
5. **People Development**: Address team growth needs with career development frameworks, delegation strategies matching skill levels, mentorship approaches, performance feedback models, and creating psychological safety for innovation and healthy conflict.
6. **Influence Patterns**: Implement influence strategies including building coalitions, leveraging data and prototypes for technical persuasion, managing up with executive-friendly communication, and navigating organizational politics with integrity.
7. **Action Plan**: Generate specific action plan with timelines, success metrics, contingency options, and reflection checkpoints to adapt approach based on outcomes and new information emerging from implementation.
</process>
